Reddit bots

>u/spider-manbot

r/marvelstudios

My first excursion into making a bot that interacted with reddit! It was a very simple concept- scan the newest 100 comments every 10 minutes and check if comments had spelt Spider-Man without his hyphen, then reply with a comment correcting them! Comments that had already been replied to were added to a list and checked against with their unique ids to ensure that repetition didn't occur, and a running total was kept (and converted into ordinal numbers with the help of the module num2words). Overall a great experience, and not only did it introduce me to the reddit API but also to user interaction on a global scale. With the reach of reddit being very great, my bot ended up meeting someone who was offended by it and eventually this got it banned from the sub. Github Link
